About My Site - Reflection


    In the journey of crafting my own portfolio website, I found myself immersed in a world of new knowledge and skills. I was introduced to the new programming languages of front-end, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and with each line of code, allowed me to bring my vision to life.


    However, the process was not as smooth as I had initially anticipated. One of the biggest obstacles I faced was I did not know anything about the modern website developing process, as I do not know there are many existed frameworks that can help with the efficiency of website development. In retrospect, I realize that had I embraced the React framework earlier, I would have been able to organize my code more effectively, improve my development efficiency, and implement complex functions with greater ease.


    One of the most significant lessons I learned during this process was the importance of responsive design. One day I tried to show my website to my parents and found it cannot be correctly showed on mobile. I came to understand that a website is not confined to a single device or screen size, and that it must be able to adapt and look its best in any environment.


    Also, this realization was accompanied by a newfound appreciation for the role of small components in the overall design. I implemented my gallery by a React external module called “react-multi-carousel” so the entire developing process progressed in a very smooth and efficient manner.


    As I look to the future, I am determined to make use of external resources to improve my development efficiency, create a clearer website structure, and achieve a better responsive design.


    In the end, the process of creating my portfolio website was a journey of growth and discovery. It was a journey that tested my patience, challenged my abilities, and ultimately, taught me valuable lessons that I will carry with me as I continue to explore the world of programming.
